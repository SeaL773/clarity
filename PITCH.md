# Clarity — Pitch Script

## The Problem (0:00)

Raise your hand if you've ever stared at a blank todo list and thought… "I know I have things to do, I just can't figure out where to start."

That's the reality for over 366 million people living with ADHD. The hardest part isn't doing the work — it's the planning. Traditional todo apps don't help. They require you to *already* be organized.

## The Solution (0:40)

**Clarity** is an AI-powered smart todo list where you just dump your thoughts. Type whatever's on your mind — messy, unstructured, stream of consciousness.

Hit one button. That's it.

## How It Works (1:00)

A **three-step AI pipeline** — not a single API call.

1. **Parse** — Extract every task, even implicit ones ("I need to eat healthier" → "plan meals", "go grocery shopping")
2. **Plan** — Prioritize with the Eisenhower Matrix, quick wins first to build momentum
3. **Summarize** — Encouraging daily recap, always leading with what you accomplished

## Key Features (1:40)

- Completed tasks gently sink — no guilt
- Sub-tasks auto-complete parent tasks
- Celebration moments at milestones (⚡🔥🎉) — real dopamine hits
- Calendar view with urgency progress bars
- Voice input via OpenAI Whisper
- Gentle daily notifications
- Zero time pressure by design

## Tech (2:20)

Flutter frontend, FastAPI backend, Claude Haiku 4.5 powering three dedicated prompts. Multi-provider LLM fallback. Rate limiting, input validation, atomic transactions. Built in one day with Kiro IDE.

## Impact (2:50)

366 million people have ADHD. 10% of American college students. Every design decision — calm colors, encouraging language, quick-wins-first ordering — is rooted in **behavioral activation therapy**.

You don't need to have it all figured out. Just tell Clarity what's on your mind. We'll figure out the rest.
